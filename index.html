Privacy Policy for vybes

Last updated: October 18, 2025

Thank you for using vybes (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in using Google, we collect certain information from your Google account through Firebase Authentication, including:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google profile picture
  • Your unique user ID (UID) assigned by Firebase

This information is used solely to create and manage your account within the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your identity and allow you to sign in securely
  • Personalize your in-app experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your information is securely stored by Firebase Authentication, a service provided by Google LLC. Firebase uses industry-standard security measures to protect your data. For more information, please refer to:

  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention and Deletion

You may delete your account and associated information at any time by contacting us at vybes.apps@gmail.com. Once deleted, your data will be permanently removed from our systems and Firebase.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at https://vybes.github.io.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: vybes.apps@gmail.com

8. Data Deletion

Users can request the deletion of their account and all associated personal data (including email address and Firebase User ID) by contacting us at vybes.apps@gmail.com. Upon receiving a request, we will permanently delete the user’s data from our database and Firebase Authentication. We are also working on an account deletion option from within the app.
