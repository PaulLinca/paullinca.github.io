Last updated: October 18, 2025

Thank you for using vybes (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

When you sign in using Google, we collect certain information from your Google account through Firebase Authentication, including:

  • Your name

  • Personalize your in-app experience

Your information is securely stored using Firebase Authentication provided by Google LLC.

Vybes is intended for users aged 16 and older.

Court Score does not collect personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by the user.
