Privacy Policy for vybes

Last updated: October 18, 2025

Thank you for using vybes (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in using Google, we collect certain information from your Google account through Firebase Authentication, including:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google profile picture
  • Your unique user ID (UID)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your identity
  • Personalize your in-app experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your information is securely stored using Firebase Authentication provided by Google LLC.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

You may delete your account by contacting us at vybes.apps@gmail.com.

5. Children’s Privacy

Vybes is intended for users aged 16 and older.

6. Contact

Email: vybes.apps@gmail.com

Privacy Policy for Court Score

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Thank you for using Court Score (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Court Score is an offline app for tracking padel match scores. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive information. No internet connection, location data, or third-party services are required.

Data Privacy

All match data is stored locally on your device. Nothing leaves your device unless you manually export it. We do not share your data with anyone.

Children’s Privacy

Court Score is suitable for all ages. No personal information is collected from children.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at vybes.apps@gmail.com.
